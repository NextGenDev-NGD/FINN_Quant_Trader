Question 1 of 10
Pattern recognition is the first skill. The edge comes from knowing context — what precedes the candle matters more than the shape itself. A hammer after a three-week downtrend at major support is a trade. A hammer in the middle of a range is noise. Never trade the pattern alone. Always ask: what is the IC on this setup, in this context, in this ticker's history?
