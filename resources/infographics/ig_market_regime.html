Regime Change — What to Watch For
The regime doesn't flip overnight — these are the early signals.
Bull → Choppy / Bear
First LL on daily after HH/HL run · VIX spike above 20 · 50d SMA slope flattening · Breadth dropping below 60% above 50d · Defensive rotation begins (XLU outperforming XLK)
⚠ Don't short the first crack — wait for LH confirmation before regime call.
Bear → Choppy / Bull
First HH on daily after LH/LL run · VIX drops below 25 · Price reclaims 50d SMA on volume · Breadth recovering above 40% · Growth rotation begins (XLK outperforming XLU)
⚠ Don't go long at the first bounce — bear market rallies are violent. Wait for structure confirmation.
The regime is the context that makes every other signal meaningful or meaningless. A breakout signal in a bull market has IC > 0.05. The exact same breakout in a choppy market has IC near 0. Wrong regime, wrong signals, wrong side. Identify the regime first. Every session. No exceptions.
