FINN Course Position
Why Swing + Quant
Capital-accessible. $1K–$5K generates real signal while avoiding the PDT rule.
Backtestable. Multi-day holds give statistically valid IC measurement — enough data points to trust the output.
Automation-ready. Webull paper API + Python signals close the loop without HFT infrastructure.
What FINN Does Not Teach
Scalping. Commission drag makes retail scalping a losing game. Burnout risk is extreme.
Pure day trading. PDT rule blocks retail below $25K. Focus shifts to swing horizon.
Discretionary only. Every entry must be codeable. Gut-feel trades are noise until quantified.
