FINN Non-Negotiable Risk Rules
Rule 1
2%
Max risk per trade. One bad trade cannot significantly damage the account.
Rule 2
6%
Max portfolio drawdown. Three losing trades at 2% = pause and review.
Rule 3
2:1
Minimum R:R before entering. Don't risk $1 to make less than $2.
Rule 4
1.5× ATR
Stop-loss placement minimum. Inside the noise = stopped out by normal volatility.
Rule 5
20%
Max single-position size as % of account. Concentration risk kills accounts.
Rule 6
Log All
Every trade: entry, exit, size, rationale, outcome. No log = no edge development.
Position sizing is not optional math — it's the mechanism that keeps you in the game long enough to let your edge compound. A system with IC = 0.06 and 2% risk per trade survives a 30-trade losing streak. The same system with 10% risk per trade is bankrupt in 7 losses. The edge doesn't matter if you can't survive the variance.
